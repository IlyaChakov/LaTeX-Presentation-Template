\section{Conclusion}

\begin{frame}
  \frametitle{Results of the research}
  \begin{enumerate}
    \item Concept of an economic cycle is defined, approaches to its research and definition are systematized;
    \item Key elements of the cycle theory are highlighted, the analysis and systematization of existing theoretical concepts are carried out on their basis;
    \item Role of the industrial cycle as a specific form of dialectical development of the capitalist economy is justified;
    \item Process of reproduction of fixed capital as a key factor of the industrial cycle is described and modeled;
    \item Concrete historical analysis of crises and cycles in the US economy has been carried out;
    \item Empirical analysis of the dynamics of indicators and cycle measures in the US economy has been performed.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
